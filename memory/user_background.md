The user designs concurrent systems inspired by neuroscience concepts they've formalized. They think about the system as 3D spatial structures (spiral hierarchies, multiple timelines) and reason about details by mentally zooming into specific parts rather than viewing everything at once.

They understand the tradeoffs of LLM architecture (attention degradation, representational capacity, autocomplete vs reasoning) and are pragmatic about what AI tools can and can't do.
